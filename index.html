Made by Hand

Founded in a tiny home kitchen, Sweet Crumb was born out of a deep respect for slow, simple methods. We believe that cakes shouldn't be overly complicated or full of artificial colorings.

Every layer is baked fresh daily using local grain flour, real pasture-raised eggs, and fruits sourced from neighborhood farms. Pure flavor, intentional details.

Common Questions

Do you offer custom designs?

We work exclusively within our signature minimalist, rustic aesthetic. While we don't do fondant sculpture work, we love adding custom flower toppings or handwritten inscriptions.

How far in advance should I order?

As a boutique small batch kitchen, we suggest booking custom sizes at least 5 days in advance. Standard sizes from our daily menu can often be ordered 48 hours prior.
